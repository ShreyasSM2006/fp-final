Who are we?

Vatsalya Trust is a public charitable trust dedicated to supporting orphaned and underprivileged children...

Vatsalya Trust, Mumbai is registered under the Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950. The organization focuses on care, protection, and empowerment of destitute children and marginalized youth.
